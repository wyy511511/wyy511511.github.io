My research focuses on empowering individuals to transform emerging technologies into personal tools that cater to their unique needs and goals. This can be summarized in three key aspects:

  • Enhancing interactivity and engagement: Leveraging technology to support personalized learning and education.
  • Ensuring safety and privacy: Developing usable solutions to build trust and security in technology.
  • Improving accessibility: Making technology inclusive and beneficial to a wider audience.

To achieve these goals, I leverage diverse methodologies and prototypes, including but not limited to Multimodal Large Language Models, mobile development, sensors, XR/AR, crowdsourcing, and social media analytics.
